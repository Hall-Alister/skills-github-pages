title: Hugh McAlister
---

Hugh McAlister was born 27th of May 2012 in rural Sydney, Australia. As a child he dreamt of being Prime Minister, until deciding he wanted to work as an Intelligence Officer for the Australian Secret Intelligence Service when he was 13. 

Hugh enjoys reading a variety of fiction genres like dystopia, futuristic, classic, sci-fi, thriller, horror, and general fiction. He also reads many non-fiction books like the Power Broker by Robert Caro, and The Art of War by Sun Tzu. He enjoys learning and often spends his free time developing a new skill like GitHub proficiency. 

Hugh is a devout Christian and hopes to continue living by the Lord's example as he continues into the next great chapters of his life.
